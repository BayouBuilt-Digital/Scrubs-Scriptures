# CLAUDE.md — project notes for future sessions

## What this repo is

A **business playbook** delivered by **BayouBuilt Digital** (a service of
D&G Fuzion LLC) to a specific client, Wanda Pizzolato, LPN. The subject
matter touches faith + healthcare because that's the client's niche — but
**the deliverable itself is a business document, not a devotional.**
Do not describe it or its voice as devotional, liturgical, pastoral, or
any similar framing.

When writing copy for this repo, default to:
- Warm but plainspoken
- Business-forward (playbook, guide, advisory)
- Voice reference: "Come Say Hello," "Start a Conversation,"
  "Schedule a Free Site Walkthrough" — the CTA copy already in the site

## Working conventions

- Source at repo root (`index.html`, `styles.css`, `script.js`); minified
  deploy bundle in `/dist` regenerated by `python3 tools/build.py`
- PDF regenerated by `node tools/make-pdf.js`
- All development on a feature branch → PR → squash or merge to `main`
- Don't change visible copy, voice, or design without explicit approval
